Introduction

The CADD model permits the transfer of dislocations across the interface between the atomistic and continuum domains. In the atomistic domain, either molecular dynamics (MD) or molecular statics (MS) method are employed; in the continuum domain, the discrete dislocation dynamics is used to give dislocation trajectories via elastic interactions between dislocations.
